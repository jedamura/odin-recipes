Lasagna Recipe

This This Lasagna Recipe is easy to make with a creamy ricotta cheese mixture, savory meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients

Cheese Filling

  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • 2 tsp Itlian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper

Meat Sauce

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, finely diced
  • 3/4 lb ground beef
  • 3/4 lb ground Italian sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 40 oz marinara sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp hot Sauce
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce

Lasagna

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2.5 cups mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Combine cheese filling ingredients
  2. Combine Meat Sauce ingredients
  3. Layer both mixtures between the 12 lasagna noodles
  4. Bake for 35 minutes at 375 degrees
